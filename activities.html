Activities

Our Activities

Engage your body, mind, and spirit with immersive experiences designed to bring joy, peace, and a deep connection to nature.

Tapovan Happiness Valley isn’t just a place—it’s a celebration of life in its most natural form. Surrounded by forested hills, organic farms, and whispering winds, Tapovan is Kerala's emerging eco-retreat where happiness grows like jackfruit on trees.

Our thoughtfully curated activities invite visitors to disconnect from screens and stress and reconnect with soil, soul, and sunshine. Whether you're a solo traveler, a wellness seeker, or a family on a weekend getaway — Tapovan promises moments of joy, reflection, and meaningful bonding.

Tapovan Happiness Valley offers a wide range of nature-based and recreational activities designed to reconnect visitors with the outdoors. Guests can wander through lush jackfruit forests, vibrant butterfly gardens, and eco trails as part of our immersive nature walks. Each morning begins with energizing yoga and meditation sessions held in serene, oxygen-rich surroundings, helping you reset and refocus.

For those looking to unwind and have fun, the rain dance and natural mud bath experiences offer pure, earthy joy. Horse riding is available for all age groups, guided by trained staff, providing an exciting way to explore the landscape. Guests can also enjoy traditional archery and peaceful fishing sessions — perfect for families, children, and anyone seeking a mindful connection to nature.

Morning Yoga

Begin your day with revitalizing yoga sessions guided by certified instructors. Performed in serene open-air settings surrounded by greenery, these sessions enhance flexibility, balance, and mental clarity.

Rain Dance

Dance your heart out under simulated rainfall and groovy Afro-European music. A favorite among families and children, this refreshing experience is pure joy on a hot day.

Horse Riding

Ride through scenic paths at our well-maintained horse stable. Suitable for beginners and enthusiasts, with safety gear and trained handlers provided for a memorable riding experience.

Nature Walks

Explore butterfly gardens, medicinal plant trails, and jackfruit groves on guided eco-walks. Learn about biodiversity while reconnecting with the sights and sounds of nature.

Archery

Sharpen your focus and aim with traditional archery. Safe zones, quality bows, and expert guidance make this a thrilling activity for kids and adults alike.

Target Shooting

Experience the excitement of air-rifle target shooting in a controlled and secure environment. Perfect for those looking to improve accuracy and enjoy a competitive activity.

Fishing

Relax by the natural pond and try your hand at fishing. We provide all the equipment and assistance needed to enjoy a peaceful, meditative experience in nature.
