About

The Story Behind Tapovan — Preserving Culture, Cultivating Wellness

Tapovan Happiness Valley is Kerala’s one-of-a-kind wellness and recreational retreat. Built on values of sustainability, serenity, and celebration, we offer families and travelers a chance to reconnect with nature and themselves.

Located in a scenic landscape surrounded by lush greenery, Tapovan is more than a destination — it’s an experience rooted in cultural richness, joy, and rejuvenation.

  • Nature-Integrated Design: Experience healing spaces like the Jackfruit Forestorium and Butterfly Bio Park.
  • Holistic Wellness: Yoga, mud therapy, and stress-relief programs led by wellness experts.
  • Family-Friendly Activities: Kids parks, rain dance, swimming, and farm stays for all age groups.
  • Community & Culture: Host weddings, cultural events, and retreats in our scenic outdoor venues.

Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before visiting Tapovan Happiness Valley – nature's sanctuary for peace, fun, and wellness.

Where is Tapovan Happiness Valley located?

Tapovan is situated just 20 km east of Kollam city, Kerala. It is easily accessible from Kottarakkara (14 km), Ayur (8 km), and Thiruvananthapuram (58 km). You can find directions by searching "Tapovan Happiness Valley" on Google Maps.

Do I need to book in advance to visit or stay at Tapovan?

While walk-in visitors are welcome, we highly recommend advance booking for accommodations, destination weddings, event space, or spa sessions to ensure availability and personalized service.

Is Tapovan family and child-friendly?

Absolutely! Tapovan features a vibrant Kids Park, baby pool, trampoline jump, cycling trails, animal interactions, and more — making it perfect for family outings and school excursions.

What health and wellness services are offered?

Tapovan offers yoga, meditation, scientific stress-relief programs, fish pedicures, mud baths, and trained swimming sessions — all set within our lush green environment designed to promote holistic wellness.

Are food and refreshments available inside?

Yes. We have a European-standard cafeteria offering sugarcane juice, jackfruit dishes, local snacks, and beverages — all surrounded by nature and right beside the pool and play zones.
