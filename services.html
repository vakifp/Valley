Our Services

Tapovan Happiness Valley offers a unique blend of nature, wellness, education, and adventure services designed for all age groups and interests.

Jack Forestorium

India’s first Jackfruit Forestorium with over 600 trees spread across 6 acres—an oxygen paradise that supports eco-tourism and wellness.

Stress Relief Centre

Rejuvenate with yoga, meditation, and counseling under trained wellness coaches—perfect for urban stress detox.

Mud Bath & Rain Dance

Enjoy a natural mud therapy experience or dance away your stress under Afro-European beats and simulated rain.

Community Hall

A multipurpose space with dining facilities, perfect for cultural programs, family events, or public gatherings.

Swimming Training

Safe and certified swimming lessons for kids and adults in a serene pool setting—with separate baby pool.

Destination Weddings

Celebrate your big day in a lush, natural setting with open-air dining, floral décor, and hill-view backdrops.

Farm Stay & Cafeteria

Relax in eco-tents or A/C rooms, and dine on fresh jackfruit dishes, sugarcane juice, and other organic snacks.

Event Hosting

Tapovan’s open-air amphitheater and scenic landscapes are perfect for performances, school tours, and spiritual retreats.

Tapovan Happiness Valley is home to India’s first Jackfruit Forestorium — a six-acre eco forest that promotes wellness and biodiversity awareness.

Our Stress Relief Centre offers guided yoga, meditation, and wellness programs designed to help you relax and recharge in nature.

The Mud Bath and Rain Dance zone brings playful therapy to life, letting you embrace nature while releasing stress through immersive experiences.

We provide cozy farm stay accommodations with both AC and non-AC rooms. Guests can enjoy organic jackfruit-based meals and traditional Kerala refreshments at our café.

Our swimming pool is ideal for families, with certified instructors offering swimming lessons for children and adults in a safe, scenic environment.

Tapovan is also a preferred destination for weddings, get-togethers, and school retreats, offering stunning hilltop views and open-air event spaces.

Our outdoor amphitheater and community-friendly design support cultural programs, spiritual retreats, and recreational gatherings year-round.
