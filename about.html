The Story Behind Tapovan — Preserving Culture, Cultivating Wellness

Where the whisper of the forest meets the rhythm of wellness — experience life in its most natural and joyful form.

Tapovan Happiness Valley is rooted in history and inspired by vision. This sacred land once served as the capital of King Veliyanan during the 5th century and became the heartland of socio-political reform movements. With a commitment to sustainable living, scientific jackfruit cultivation was introduced here by clearing rubber plantations — leading to the establishment of a thriving oxygen forest with over 600 jackfruit trees.

Tapovan Happiness Valley is a nature-inspired wellness destination located in Veliyam, Kerala — a place once ruled by King Veliyanan and now transformed into India’s first Jack Forestorium.

  • 600+ jackfruit trees spread over 6 acres — an oxygen-rich forest promoting health and biodiversity.
  • Stunning entrance modeled after Bali’s ‘Hand of God’ gate — a spiritual and architectural marvel.
  • Home to India's first complete bio-park featuring wellness programs, meditation centers, and kid-friendly activities.

Tapovan is more than just a destination — it's a sanctuary where nature, culture, and wellness come together. From guided meditation to community gatherings, from butterfly parks to mud baths, every experience is designed to heal the body and elevate the spirit.
